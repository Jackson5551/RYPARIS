HAMBURGER OF THE WEEK

Time

Level: Easy Prep: 10 min Yield: 4 Hamburgers
Total: 25 min Cook: 15 min

BENGAL BURGER

★★★★★ 26 Reviews
Save Recipie🖨

"No more dry, lackluster burgers. These are juicy, and spices can be easily added or changed to suit anyone's taste. Baste frequently with your favorite barbeque sauce. If you find the meat mixture too mushy, just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold their shape."

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces ground chuck
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black peper
  • Spice Mix, optional, recipe follows
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 slices of cheese, such as Cheddar, American cheese
  • 4 soft hamburger-style buns, split
  • 4 slices beefsteak tomatoes, optional
  • Assorted lettuces and greens, such as iceburd
  • Assorted mustards, optional
  • Mayonnaise, optional
  • Ketchup, optional

    • Spice Mix

  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons oinion powder
  • Hot Sauce, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F. Place a roasting rack on a foil-lined baking sheet in the oven

  2. Using vour hands. break the meat into small pieces and combine evenly out loosely on a parchment or waxed paper-lined baking sheet Spread the meat out and season it generously with salt and pepper. If desired, add the spice mix at this time.

  3. Divide the meat into 4 portions (about 6 ounces each). Using your hands, form each portion into a ball-shape by gently tossing it from 1 hand to the other. (Don't over work or press too firmly on the meat.) Gently form each portion into a patty about 3 1/2 inches wide and 1-inch-thick.

  4. Preheat a large cast iron skillet over medium-low heat for 5 minutes. Raise the heat to high and add the oil. Add the patties and cook. turning once. until well-browned. about 2 minutes each side.

  5. Using a spatula, transfer the hamburgers to the roasting rack in the oven and continue cookina to desired doneness. 8 to 9 minutes for medium-rare. 10 to 11 minutes for medium. and 13 to 15 minutes for well-done. If you are using the cheese, top the hampurgers during their last couple of minutes of cookina to melt

  6. Transfer the hamburgers to a plate, let rest for a couple minutes before serving. Meanwhile, toast the hamburger buns. Assemble the hamburgers with the condiments and topings of your choice. Serve

  7. When cooking on a countertoop grill. turn on grill, then place burgers on the heated grill.

    8. Timing for a countertop grill:

  8. For Medium-Rare: Cook for 3 minutes covered and then unplug the machine and continue to cook covered for 2 to 2 more minutes

  9. For Medium: Cook for 4 minutes covered and then unolug the machine and conimle to cook covered for 4 more minutes

  10. For Well: Cook for 5 minutes covered and then unplug the machine.
